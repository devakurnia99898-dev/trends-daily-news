**Last Updated: January 2026**

At **trends Daily**, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by trends Daily and how we use it.

## Log Files
trends Daily follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, ISP, date/time stamp, and referring/exit pages. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

## Cookies and Web Beacons
trends Daily uses 'cookies' to store information including visitors' preferences and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our content based on visitors' browser type.

## Google DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google is a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

## Advertising Partners & GDPR/CCPA
Our advertising partners (such as **Google AdSense**) may use cookies and web beacons. Each partner has their own Privacy Policy. We are committed to complying with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA regulations regarding user data transparency.

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
